/ 01   Process

From Complaint
to Resolution.

A streamlined four-step process that ensures every civic complaint reaches the right authority — fast and transparently.

01
📝

Smart Submission

Citizens submit complaints via our web portal. AI pre-fills category and department based on description, reducing errors.

02
🤖

AI Classification

Our AI engine auto-categorizes issues, detects urgency level, and routes the complaint to the correct department within seconds.

03
📡

Real-Time Tracking

Citizens track complaint status live — Submitted → Accepted → In Review → Resolved — with full transparency at every step.

04
✅

Transparent Resolution

Authorities resolve and document outcomes. Citizens receive confirmation and a resolution timeline for complete accountability.
